How Did I Start Making Jewelry?

I started making jewelry when I was 14 years old. I was on vacation at the time, and I met a woman who sold jewelry and beads for a living. She taught me how to make my first necklace and pair of earrings. After that day, I knew that this would become one of my passions. I have changed my style over the years since then. Every single piece of jewelry is unique and different!

Making Jewelry is so Fun!
